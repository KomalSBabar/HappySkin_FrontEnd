All patients approved for Accutane will be enrolled in the iPledge program and will be prompted to get bloodwork done after 2 months into treatment.


Female patients are required to either pledge abstinence or be on two forms of approved birth control during treatment. Female patients must also take monthly pregnancy tests every month (which can be done at home) and upload the results to the portal after their doctor approves the monthly prescription as well as answer monthly questions on the ipledgeprogram.com website.

Per iPledge requirements for female patients, if approved for Accutane by your assigned dermatologist after submitting this evaluation, there will be a 30 day mandatory "wait period" before receiving your first supply of pills.
