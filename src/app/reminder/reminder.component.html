Reminder: iPledge Requirements for Patients Who Can Get Pregnant (Female Patients)

Be prepared to take pregnancy tests monthly

If approved for Accutane, you’ll need to upload a photo of your first negative pregnancy test to be enrolled into the iPledge program. Pregnancy tests can be purchased from your local drugstore.

30-day “wait period” if approved for treatment

Female patients must undergo an initial 30-day “wait period” before they can submit a follow-up evaluation and a second pregnancy test to receive the first supply of pills.
